Mushroom Stew

The Mushroom Stew from minecraft is a tasty dish that is all organic. Made from freshly picked shrooms, this stew fills 3 full Hunger Points! Today I will teach you how to make it for yourself!

Ingredients Needed


  • Red Mushroom


  • Brown Mushroom


  • Wooden Bowl

The Process

  1. Gather One Red Mushroom and One Brown Mushroom

  2. Build a Wooden Bowl

  3. Place the Bowl and Mushrooms into a crafting table

  4. Combine everything together

  5. Enjoy your freshly made Mushroom Stew!
